📜 Manifesto

Hello! I’m Pasha, a first-generation Russian American graphic artist and web developer. My interest in software engineering ignited during Springboard’s coding bootcamp in 2022. Building websites allows me to blend my intellectual and artistic passions. Currently based in Pacific Grove, CA, I’m actively seeking full-stack or frontend roles. I aspire to thrive in a creative, ambitious, and collaborative work environment.

Presently, I work as a web developer at eWebsiteServices—a website design firm and SEO team in Danville, CA. Additionally, I volunteer with Vegan Hacktivists, providing free web development services to vegan and organizations. Independently, I assist small businesses in Monterey County to enhance their web presence. As a lifelong learner, I advocate passionately for the environment, animal rights, and social justice.
